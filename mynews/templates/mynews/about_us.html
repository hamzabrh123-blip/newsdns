About Us

Uttar World: Keeping an Eye on Every Pulse of Uttar Pradesh

What is Uttar World?

Uttar World (uttarworld.com) is an independent and unbiased digital news portal dedicated to the people of Uttar Pradesh and Hindi-speaking audiences worldwide. In today’s fast-paced digital era, we are committed to delivering 'Truth' and 'Accuracy'. Through our platform, we give a voice to local stories and grassroots issues that often go unnoticed in mainstream media. For us, journalism is not just about information—it’s a responsibility toward society.

Our Story

Uttar World began with a simple vision: to give a digital identity to local issues. We realized that ground-level reporting from districts like Bahraich, Gonda, Shravasti, Balrampur, and Sitapur was missing in the digital space. To bridge this gap, we launched this platform. Our journey started with providing simple updates on government schemes, railway changes, and local events. Today, we continue to grow thanks to the trust of our readers.

Our Vision

Our goal is to become the most trusted and accessible news network in Uttar Pradesh. We believe in independent journalism, free from political or commercial pressure. Our vision is to empower every citizen by providing transparent, high-quality news in their own language, helping them stay informed and make better decisions for their lives and communities.

What We Cover

Railway & Transport

Latest Indian Railway updates, new train schedules, and essential passenger information.

Education & Jobs

Updates on government jobs (UPPSC, UPSSSC), results, and career guidance for youth.

Local Governance

Deep dive into local administration, social issues, and community developments.

Editorial Integrity

At Uttar World, we strictly adhere to Google Publisher Policies. Our content is 100% original, and we do not support or practice plagiarism. Every news story passes through a strict fact-checking protocol before publication. For us, the trust of our readers is more valuable than any advertising revenue.
