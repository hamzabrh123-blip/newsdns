Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 26, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Uttar World (https://uttarworld.com). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you visit our website.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect two types of information when you interact with our site:

  • Personal Information: This includes your name and email address if you voluntarily provide them via our 'Contact Us' forms or comment sections.
  • Non-Personal Information: We use log files that include IP addresses, browser types, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date/time stamps, and number of clicks. This data is not linked to any personally identifiable information.

3. Cookies and Web Beacons

Like any other website, Uttar World uses 'cookies'. These cookies are used to store information including visitors' preferences, and the pages on the website that the visitor accessed or visited. The information is used to optimize the users' experience by customizing our web page content.

4. Google DoubleClick DART Cookie

Google is one of the third-party vendors on our site. It also uses cookies, known as DART cookies, to serve ads to our site visitors based upon their visit to our site and other sites on the internet. However, visitors may choose to decline the use of DART cookies by visiting the Google ad and content network Privacy Policy.

5. Advertising Partners

Third-party ad servers or ad networks use technologies like cookies, JavaScript, or Web Beacons that are used in their respective advertisements and links that appear on Uttar World. They automatically receive your IP address when this occurs. These technologies are used to measure the effectiveness of their advertising campaigns.

6. Data Security

We prioritize the security of your data. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, remember that no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

7. Children's Information

Protecting children's privacy is important to us. Uttar World does not knowingly collect any Personal Identifiable Information from children under the age of 13. If you think your child provided this kind of information on our website, we strongly encourage you to contact us immediately, and we will do our best to promptly remove such information from our records.

8. Consent

By using our website, you hereby consent to our Privacy Policy and agree to its Terms and Conditions.
